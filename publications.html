Zhiguo Yang

Associate Professor, School of Mathematical Sciences, Shanghai Jiao Tong University. Research interests include structure-preserving spectral and spectral-element methods, multiscale PDEs, electromagnetic-kinetic transport, liquid crystals, and moiré materials.
